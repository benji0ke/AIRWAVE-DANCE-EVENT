About Us

Who We Are

Airwave is a community-led dance event bringing together dancers, DJs, and judges to celebrate performance, choreography and music. Started as a school project, the event focuses on inclusion, learning and showcasing local talent.

Our Mission

We aim to create an affordable, welcoming platform where emerging dancers can perform, receive constructive feedback, and grow their networks. We prioritise accessibility, safety, and a supportive environment for all skill levels.
